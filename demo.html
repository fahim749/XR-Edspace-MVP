Living Textbook - Augmented Reality MVP Demo

Welcome to the Future of Education

XR_EdSpace transforms traditional textbooks into interactive 3D learning experiences using augmented reality technology. This MVP demonstrates how students can scan a printed diagram and instantly see a fully interactive 3D anatomical model appear on their smartphone.

🚀 How to Experience the AR Demo

  1. Print the Marker: Print the target image below or display it on another screen
  2. Open on Mobile: Access this demo on your smartphone or tablet
  3. Launch AR: Click the "Launch AR Experience" button below
  4. Grant Camera Access: Allow the browser to use your camera when prompted
  5. Point & Discover: Point your camera at the printed marker to see the 3D heart model
  6. Interact: Touch to rotate, pinch to zoom, and explore the anatomy

🎓 The Living Textbook Vision

XR_EdSpace partners with textbook publishers to embed AR markers directly into educational materials. Students scan diagrams with their smartphones to access interactive 3D models, animations, and supplementary content - transforming passive reading into active exploration.

🎯 Target Market & Business Model

Primary Markets

Higher Education: Universities and colleges seeking innovative teaching tools for anatomy, engineering, chemistry, and other spatial subjects.

K-12 Education: Schools looking to enhance STEM education with engaging, interactive content that improves retention and understanding.

Professional Training: Medical schools, nursing programs, and technical training institutions requiring hands-on learning experiences.

Revenue Model

Publisher Partnerships: License AR content creation tools to major educational publishers (Pearson, McGraw-Hill, Wiley) for integration into existing textbooks.

Institutional Licensing: Subscription-based access for universities and schools, priced per student or per course.

Custom Content Development: Create bespoke AR experiences for specialized educational programs and corporate training.
